WALL-E

Small, compact, and great personality. Here is our little friend Wall-E. With WiFi control capabilites and live video feed, this little guy can be drivin without even needing to see him. He is easy to build and even easier to use.

Our goals for this project were to develop a Wall-E rover. To have a robot that held some human characteristics, was drivable via mobile phone, and could send back live video footage. Below is the the system diagram depicting the relationships between the Subsystems
