Sprint Two

This was our second sprint review, which occured on November 15th, 2022.

Sprint Two Overview

At this point, our MPV plan was still to have a spring-loaded marker mechanism that would allow us to draw a design on a pumpkin, and our reach goal was still to have a mechanism that could raster an image onto a pumpkin.

The current state of our project was as follows:

  • For our mechanical design, we had a fully functional z-axis with a spring-loaded marker carriage, though we were currently experiencing some issues with the threaded rod. We also had a fully functional rotating base.
  • For our electical design, we had limit switches that were wired but not yet programmed. Our motors were wired and connected to the CNC shield, and the power input to the CNC shield was completed.
  • For our software, we only had a functioning stepper motor for the main base, which allowed the plate to spin.

Current State Mechanical

Current State Electrical

In terms of our biggest technical risk, we were currently dealing with risks in the software element. We were dealing with issues in our GRBL program, but we passed it off as us not yet understanding the program, and we truly believed that we just need to properly test our code and make some nessary adjustments to achieve our goals.

At this point our target goal still had not changed, and we still hoped to integrate a laser into our design if time-permitting. Therefore, we hoped that by the next sprint to have our limit switches working, integrate in our E-stop, and also have our drawing software somewhat working.
